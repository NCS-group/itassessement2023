BOOK REVIEW Life 3.0: Being Human in the Age of Artificial Intelligence

“This is the most important conversation of our time, and Tegmark’s thought-provoking book will help you join it.” — Stephen Hawking “Intelligence is simply the ability to solve complex tasks—or so” - Max Tegmark I was very much fascinated by this book. Life 3.0 does not have the same constraint compared with Life 1.0 & 2.0, Tegmark writes—calling it as the “master of its own destiny.” We once figured out only human beings could make realistic painting, play music and drive vehicles. Today, we have AI that can do all these and significantly more. All that can be instilled with AI can be permeated with AI. Diminish Diamandi calls attention to AI which will largely affect the human condition than the invention of electricity or fire. That is an amazing thought. Artificial intelligence is set to change work, society, culture, our bodies and even less manpower.
